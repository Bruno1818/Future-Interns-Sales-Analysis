# Business Sales Performance Dashboard (Task 1) - Future Interns

## 📌 Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the Future Interns Data Science & Analytics Internship. The goal was to transform raw, unorganized sales data into an interactive, executive-level dashboard to drive business decision-making.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Microsoft Excel**: Data Cleaning, Pivot Tables, and Dashboard Design.
- **Power Query / Formulas**: Used for currency normalization and calculating Profit Margins.

## 🧹 Data Cleaning Process
To ensure the analysis was "client-ready," I performed the following:
- **Currency Normalization**: Converted text-based sales and profit strings into numeric floats.
- **Date Formatting**: Standardized order dates to allow for Year-over-Year trend analysis.
- **Calculated Fields**: Added a 'Profit Margin %' helper column to identify operational efficiency.
- **Data Integrity**: Used `TRIM` functions and removed duplicates to ensure 100% accuracy.

## 📊 Key Business Insights
1. **Growth Trend**: Revenue reached a peak of **$733,215 in 2017**, showing a **20.36% increase** from 2016.
2. **Profit Leakage**: While **Tables** generate significant sales, they operate at a **-8.56% profit margin**, representing a major area for cost optimization.
3. **Regional Powerhouse**: The **West Region** is the most profitable territory, contributing **37.8%** of total company earnings.
4. **Product MVP**: **Phones** are the top-performing sub-category, balancing high volume ($330k) with strong margins (13.5%).

## 💡 Recommendations
- **Pricing Strategy**: Increase margins on 'Tables' and 'Bookcases' through price adjustments or shipping cost reductions.
- **Inventory Focus**: Prioritize stock and marketing for high-margin categories like **Technology** (16.7% avg margin).
